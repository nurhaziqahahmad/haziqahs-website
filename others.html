GLOSSARY
Microsoft Office
Microsoft Office or simply Office is a discontinued family of client software, server software, and services developed by Microsoft.

HTML
The HyperText Markup Language or HTML is the standard markup language for documents designed to be displayed in a web browser.

ADOBE
Adobe Inc. originally called Adobe Systems Incorporated is an American multinational computer software company.

KALAM JAMAIE
Kalam Jamaie is choral speaking but uses Arabic. Kalam Jamaie has now been introduced throughout the states in Malaysia and there are state-level competitions between schools in Malaysia. Now, it is run by J-QAF teachers in their schools.

UPSR
Primary School Achievement Test also known as Ujian Pencapaian Sekolah Rendah was a national examination taken by all students in Malaysia at the end of their sixth year in primary school before they leave for secondary school.

PT3
Summative assessment to assess the academic achievement of students at the junior secondary level in Malaysia. PT3 is an assessment that is used to replace the Lower Secondary Assessment that has been conducted since 1993. This assessment covers the scope of the syllabus from Form 1 to Form 3.

SPM
The Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia or the Malaysian Certificate of Education is a national examination sat for by all fifth-form secondary school students in Malaysia.
